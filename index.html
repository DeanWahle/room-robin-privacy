Room Robin Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Room Robin ("the app") helps roommates split household chores fairly. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address. Passwords are stored only as salted bcrypt hashes; we never see or store your plaintext password.
  • Household content — chore definitions, schedules, assignments, votes, and activity history for households ("nests") you belong to.
  • Photos you take — before/after chore photos and optional bump photos. These are visible only to members of your nest.
  • Device push token — if you allow notifications, we store your APNs device token to send you assignment, voting, and bump notifications.

How we use it

  • Operate the core service: round-robin chore rotation, photo proof, household voting, and bumps.
  • Send push notifications you can disable at any time in iOS Settings.
  • We do not serve ads, sell your data, or use your data for tracking or profiling.

Where it's stored & who processes it

  • Supabase — database and photo storage (private bucket, time-limited signed URLs).
  • Railway — application hosting.
  • Apple Push Notification service — notification delivery.

We do not share your information with anyone else. Photos and activity are visible only to members of your own nest.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. To delete your account and associated data, email dhwahle@gmail.com and we will delete it within 30 days. Note that deleting your account does not remove content other nest members created.

Security

All traffic uses TLS. Photos live in a private storage bucket accessible only via short-lived signed URLs issued by our server to nest members.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change and continued use constitutes acceptance.
