Room Robin Support

Room Robin splits household chores fairly across your roommates — round-robin assignments, before & after photo proof, and flock approval voting.

Contact us

Questions, bugs, or account deletion requests: solongstudios@icloud.com

Common questions

How do I invite roommates?
Share your nest's invite code — it's at the top of The Nest tab.

How does rotation work?
Each chore rotates through nest members in order. Nobody gets the same chore twice in a row.

Who sees my photos?
Only members of your nest. Photos are stored privately and shared via expiring links.

How do I delete my account?
Email solongstudios@icloud.com and we'll remove it within 30 days.
